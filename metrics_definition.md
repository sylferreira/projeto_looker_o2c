# Looker Dashboard metrics (Service Level Analysis)

## KPIs

### 1. Fill Rate (%)
SUM(delivery_qty) / SUM(order_qty)

### 2. Rejection Rate (%)
SUM(rejected_qty) / SUM(order_qty)


### 3. Lead Times
- leadtime_confirmed = confirmed_delivery_date – sales_document_date  
- leadtime_agi = actual_goods_issue_date – sales_document_date
